Why should you get a detail?

Reasons why:

  • Protect your investment-whether it's a new car or a used car it is still a new car to you.
  • Prevent interior odor.
  • Prevent fading on plastics from the sun.
  • Prevent fading on your paint.
  • Protect your car paint from rocks etc.


There are many more benefits to getting a detail.
